abstract: 'The rediscovery of the holotype of <i>Stephanus</i> (<i>Distephanus</i>) [sic!] <i>athesinus</i> Biegeleben, 1929, preserved in the collections of the Museo di Storia Naturale, Venice (Italy), is recorded together with a redescription and a comment on its taxonomic identity. Therefore, its synonymy with <i>Megischus anomalipes</i> (Förster, 1855) is confirmed. New distributional data for <i>M. anomalipes</i> is provided: it is recorded for the first time from the Italian regions of Veneto, Emilia-Romagna, Tuscany and Basilicata, and newly recorded from Greece'
